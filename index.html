How does the purchase process work?

Add items to your cart, proceed to checkout, and create a ticket. A staff member will deliver your item within 24 hours directly to your Minecraft account.

Never. Staff only communicates through the official ticket system. Never trust anyone claiming to be Wind SMP staff via private messages or on alt accounts.

We accept Paysafe Card and most major payment methods. Exact options are confirmed inside your support ticket once it's opened.
